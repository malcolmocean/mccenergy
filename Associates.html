Equilibrium Engineering — now rebranding as Equilibrium Solutions — is a Nova Scotia high-performance engineering firm focused on sustainable design and Net Zero buildings. Having recently integrated the sustainable design team from Solterre, the merged firm combines technical engineering precision with environmental responsibility.

BFreeHomes

BFreeHomes is an energy-efficient home design and consulting firm specializing in sustainable building. They offer site planning, passive solar design, renovations with planning for aging-in-place, and mechanical system upgrades for new and existing homes — guiding homeowners and builders on energy efficiency, green materials, and renewable energy.
