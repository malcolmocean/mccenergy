About | MCC Energy Strategies Inc.

MCC Energy Strategies guides people (industrial, commercial, and residential) through implementation of energy saving ideas, using resources (rebates) of Efficiency NS as well as other technical experts.

MCC Energy provides an unbiased, knowledgeable advice on energy efficiency ideas that will lead to cost savings and reduction in harmful emissions - specifically in the areas of:

  • Air compressor systems
  • Energy Management Information Systems
  • Building Automation Systems for HVAC
  • Heat Pumps
  • Solar Photovoltaic Systems
  • Equipment Maintenance Programs

The technology exists for a building to have a lower carbon footprint, and to save dollars. We know you want to do the ‘right thing’ but maybe you don’t know how to put all the pieces together? We can help.

OUR MISSION

Technology exists for a building to have a lower carbon footprint, and to save dollars. Customers want to do the ‘right thing’ but don’t know how to put all the pieces together. Our mission is to help with this.

We understand the desire to save money and have set up MCC Energy to do it's part to help mitigate climate change through effective use of energy. We read the headlines, and are concerned about the future of the planet, and we know we need to act asap to turn things around. At the same time, it is important to be smart with our money.

  • Reduced use of energy, leading to cost reductions and reduced emissions
  • Facility may be more comfortable
  • Employee morale will be better
