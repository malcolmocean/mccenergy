TESTIMONIALS

"Bruce was a key person in the revitalization of the equipment at the BioVectra Facility in Windsor NS. He took charge of sophisticated pharmaceutical reactors, and successfully organized the resources and procedures so that the rebuild took place as planned. Bruce then went on to incorporate a new ‘state of the art’ Electrode De-Ionizer skid into the Ultra Purified water system and provided leadership (often hands-on) in the maintenance, troubleshooting and commissioning of the system."

Jennifer Hilborn, P.Eng, Plant Manager at BioVectra

"Good Morning, Christie, Bruce and Jacob. We owe you folks a huge thank you for putting on these workshops. An informative and enjoyable evening, benefiting from a very knowledgeable Bruce. Joe "

Joe O'Brien

"Bruce worked for me as Director Facilities at a major operation, with six large hangars and nine total buildings. Bruce showed the technical expertise and attention to detail one would expect of a Professional Engineer but more importantly he showed a passion and talent for efficiency. Bruce set the vision and executed the annual Facilities Master Plan and quickly evolved it from a corrective maintenance focus to a focus on facility improvement and cost saving initiatives. Bruce also initiated a study of the compressed air requirements for the main hanger complex, that resulted in replacement of the fixed screw air compressors with a variable speed compressor and receiver. The payback period was less than one year. Bruce prepared strong business cases, in competition with the many production projects that were being considered at that time."

Kevin Lemke Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer at Cascade Aerospace Inc.
